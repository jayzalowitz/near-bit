Under the hood

For the technically curious — here's what makes this possible.

Modified NEAR Protocol

Bitcoin Infinity is built on a fork of NEAR Protocol where Bitcoin addresses are first-class account identifiers. When you send coins to a Bitcoin address, the chain creates an account automatically.

RPC translation proxy

A lightweight Rust service sits between your wallet and the chain. It translates all 204 Bitcoin Core RPC commands into NEAR RPC calls in real time. Your wallet never knows the difference.

Native signature verification

The chain verifies Bitcoin-format secp256k1 signatures natively. When you sign a transaction with your Bitcoin key, the chain recovers your public key and verifies it matches your address.

Genesis UTXO import

A genesis builder tool reads Bitcoin's UTXO snapshot and converts every balance into a Bitcoin Infinity account. One satoshi equals 10^16 yoctoBIT, preserving full precision.
