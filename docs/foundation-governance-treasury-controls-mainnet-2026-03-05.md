## Multisig Policy

Treasury and governance signer policy:

- signer set size: 5
- threshold: 3 of 5
- signer rotation requires documented approval and published update
- emergency key revocation follows the rollback/abort protocol

## Treasury Spending Policy

- routine spending limit without emergency escalation: 50,000 USD equivalent per 30-day window
- emergency spending requires post-incident disclosure within 72 hours
- all treasury actions require multisig approval records and audit retention
